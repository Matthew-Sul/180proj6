CS180 Final Project NerF

Nerf (Neural Radiance Field) lets us train a computer generated representation of somethin given a few images and camera extrinsics and intrinsics. Part 1, has a simple MLP made to fit the pixels as we want in 2D . In Part 2, made a MLP for 3D to render the Lego.

Part 1: Fit a Neural Field to a 2D Image

Use four layer MLP with 256 hidden dimensions for the Neural Field. Get an output between 0 and 1 using Sigmoid. Has bias of low frequency features with NNs so use the sinusoidal positional encoding. Can choose L to be 4 or 10 (4 less time but less good looking tho used 10).

Adam optimizer used with lr of 1e-2, batch size 10000 (pixels), and train for 2000 iterations.
